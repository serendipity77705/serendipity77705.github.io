No one in my group had ever particpated in a hackathon, and we were all quite nervous to see how it would pan out. We said that we would do our best and if we made it to the first round then we woud be grateful. At every round we were surprised to hear our group name and be told that we would go onto the next round. Our idea was so innovative and our presentations so convincing that we somehow won first place in that hackathon. While it was a tiring weekend, it was very rewarding and taught me a lot about generative AI, API's, business models and more.

Idea

We spent a weekend working on our idea and business model, which combined both healthcare and Generative Artificial Intelligence. Our idea was to create an app that can be used by both healthcare professionals and patients to make scheduling simple and easy. We wanted to fix the issue of long waiting times and crowding in the waiting rooms of hospitals and clinics. By giving our AI model data about the business of each time period of the day, we can ensure that when a patient is scheduled they are scheduled for the best time without too much crowding. Our program would also allow easy notifications and reminders to the patient if their appointment is cancelled or rescheduled so that they don't have to wait for hours on end without realising that they won't see a physician.
